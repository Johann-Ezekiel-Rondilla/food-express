About Us

FoodExpress is a food delivery company based in Makati City, Philippines. Our company was founded in April 2025, with our primary mission being to provide safe and quick delivery options for the Filipinos' favorite foods. We have partnered with famous restaurants and fast-food chains that will be instantly recognizable to any Filipino, such as Jollibee, McDonald's, and Mang Inasal.
